# Inside Track (Warm Referrals / Tippgeber)

The **Inside Track** helps you turn personal connections — tipsters, insiders,
former colleagues, "Vitamin B" — into job opportunities, and reveals how you are
connected to a target company.

Open it from the sidebar: **Inside Track** (next to Contacts / Interviews / CRM Tasks),
at `/dashboard/referrals`.

## Recording a tip

Click **New tip**. A tip captures a warm lead that someone opened for you:

- **Tip type**
  - **Insider relay** — the tipster works *at* the target company and will pass your
    documents onward.
  - **Network path** — the tipster is not at the company but knows someone there (an
    *insider*). You can optionally name that insider.
- **Tipster** *(required)* — the contact who gave you the tip. Pick an existing
  contact (create contacts under **Contacts** first).
- **Target company** *(optional)* — set this if you already know the company. You need
  a target company later to turn the tip into a job application.

A tip with no company is fine — it is a "market tip" you can flesh out later.

## Working a referral through its lifecycle

Open a referral to see its **workspace**. A lifecycle rail shows where the tip stands:

```
open → engaged → relayed → in review → converted
```

Plus two side states: **declined** (you closed it) and **stale** (no activity for a
while — it can be revived).

Only the **next legal action** is offered at each step:

| Stage | What it means | Action |
| --- | --- | --- |
| open | tip recorded | **Engage** (send your documents / ask for an introduction) |
| engaged | you acted on it | **Relay** (documents forwarded / introduction made) |
| relayed | the target side has it | **Mark as under review** |
| in review | they are considering you | **Commit to apply** → creates a job application |
| stale | gone quiet | **Revive** |

**Decline** is available at any working stage and closes the referral.

**Commit to apply** is only possible once a **target company** is set; it creates a
real Job (in your "applied" status) linked back to this referral, which you then edit
with the real role details.

## Warm paths to a company

On a referral with a target company — and on any **Job detail** page whose company is
known — the **Warm Paths** panel shows how you can reach that company:

- **Direct insiders** — your contacts who work (or worked) there. Former employees are
  marked and are often the best door-openers.
- **Via your network** — a contact of yours who knows an insider there.

To build out the 2-hop paths, add **connections** between your contacts (who-knows-whom)
from a contact's page.

## Your privacy and your contacts' privacy

- A contact who has **withdrawn consent** is never suggested as a warm path or insider.
- If you **anonymise** a contact (Contacts → contact → anonymise), every inside-track
  link that could re-identify them is removed: their person-to-person connections are
  deleted, and any referral that named them is de-identified (and declined if it was
  still active). Referrals that already became a job keep the job but lose the name.
- A **data export** (Settings → export your data) includes your referrals and your
  contact-network connections.
